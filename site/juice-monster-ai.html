Juice Monster AI

Generative AI beverage film blending custom fluid simulation and electric color grading.

Client
Monster Energy (Concept)
Year
2025
Scope

Output

Back to Portfolio

Juice Monster AI explores the boundary between generative AI and fluid dynamics simulation in high-fidelity commercial design.

Using AI video synthesis combined with pre-rendered physical fluid layers, we achieved hyper-detailed condensation, electric juice splashes, and neon-lit color grading matching the brand's bold aesthetic.

The project serves as a showcase of commercial art direction pipelines where AI speeds up conceptualization and delivers premium, broadcast-ready visuals.
