Personal Story

For over a decade, I have operated at the intersection where technical logic meets creative intuition. My journey began in the dark rooms of traditional post-production, mastering cinematic VFX, look development, and procedural 3D systems in Houdini. I spent years refining the physics of light, texture, and motion for commercial broadcast and print campaigns.

As the visual landscape shifted, I rebuilt my practice. Today, I blend traditional high-fidelity CGI with custom generative AI architectures. By scripting bespoke pipelines and design nodes in ComfyUI, I transform raw machine compute into precise, brand-safe art direction. This hybrid workflow enables rapid visual iteration without compromising campaign-grade finish.

Now based in Ho Chi Minh City, I collaborate with directors, creative agencies, and production studios globally. Whether architecting custom visual models, designing detailed product scenes, or compositing final key visuals, I build high-impact environments that feel immersive, cohesive, and cinematic.
