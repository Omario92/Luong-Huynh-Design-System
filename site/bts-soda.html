BTS Soda

Full CGI soda campaign film with liquid simulation, backed by a complete set of unrendered 3D playblasts.

BTS Soda is a full CGI campaign film built around fresh, high-energy beverage product shots — condensation, dynamic liquid splashes and premium refraction through glass and aluminium.

The final cut is supported by a complete production set of unrendered 3D playblasts, documenting the camera moves, timing and animation passes before lighting and shading were applied.

The pipeline relied on particle physics and raytracing inside 3D suites to deliver a polished, broadcast-ready commercial finish.
