A high-energy, 15-second television commercial developed to showcase the brand's vibrant character design and packaging dynamic motion.

The workflow involved sculpting character models, rigging, and simulating high-impact animation sequences combined with commercial packaging renders.

Through advanced lighting and VFX composite pipelines, the final spot delivered a polished, broadcast-ready cinematic product presentation.
