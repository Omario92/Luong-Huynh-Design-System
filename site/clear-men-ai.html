Clear Men AI

AI-driven commercial campaign conceptualizing ice-cool freshness and styling.

Client

Year
2025
Scope
Generative AI, Conceptual Design, VFX

Back to Portfolio

Clear Men AI is a visual exploration of ice-cool freshness and clean, premium cosmetics branding using custom AI model pipelines.

Combining control-net structures and stable diffusion techniques, the workflow generates crisp, high-resolution visual frames highlighting the signature cooling mint and advanced scalp protection elements.

The pipeline showcases how generative AI can be integrated into corporate commercial adaptation, producing consistent, brand-aligned visual assets across print and digital media.
