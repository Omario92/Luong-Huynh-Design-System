Conceptual 3D modeling and scenic key visual composition developed for Vietnam Airlines' advertising campaigns.

Created three distinct thematic visual concepts (KV1, KV2, KV3) executing the complete industrial design process: starting from initial hand-drawn pencil sketches, building complex polygon meshes, setting up physical shaders, and rendering production-ready, high-resolution final outputs.

These assets highlight cargo logistics, route expansions, and modern aviation technology, deployed across nationwide print media and digital banner networks.
