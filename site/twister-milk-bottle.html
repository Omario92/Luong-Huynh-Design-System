Developed high-detail photorealistic packaging renders for the Twister Milk Bottle campaign.

The project involved precise 3D bottle modeling, custom plastic PET shaders development, and setting up clean commercial lighting to emphasize the product shape and branding artwork.

These CGI visual assets were integrated into regional print materials and digital campaign layouts, providing a highly premium and shelf-ready finish.
