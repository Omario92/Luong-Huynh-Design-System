Huda CSR

A CGI visual narrative and video production campaign developed to showcase Huda Beer's community Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives in Central Vietnam.

The workflow involved sculpting realistic environment assets, lighting community celebration scenes, and simulating water flow and landscape effects to reflect local heritage and regional pride.

The final broadcast-quality animations were integrated into multi-channel digital spots to raise public engagement and support community welfare development programs.
